Factotum*

* From the Latin fac, “do” or “make,”and tōtum, “all” or “everything”

Sofia Demopolos is a media-maker with a breadth of experience in design, photography, video, and creative strategy. Currently earning her MFA in Integrated Media Arts at Hunter College, Sofia works as an art director and graphic designer with the Greenpill Collective* and teaches Intro to Digital Media to undergraduates. She is passionate about functional design, subversive film, democracy in our daily lives, the science of resource distribution, and the art of paving a brighter future. She is based in Brooklyn.
