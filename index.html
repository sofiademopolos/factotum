Sofia Demopolos

Factotum: from the Latin, fac, "do" or "make," and tōtum, "all" or "everything."

Sofia is a media-maker with a breadth of experience in design, photography, film, and creative strategy. Currently receiving her MFA in Integrated Media Arts at Hunter College, Sofia freelances with the Greenpill Collective and teaches Intro Digital Media to undergraduates. She is passionate about progressive politics and paving a brighter future. She is based in Brooklyn.
