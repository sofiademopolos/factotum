Melville House

About

Melville House is a small, independent publisher founded in 2001. Their New York office is based in the DUMBO neighborhood of Brooklyn.

My Role

I was inspired to apply for a role at Melville House when I saw that they had published Debt: The First 5,000 Years, by David Graeber, one of my favorite books. I worked in the company's design and production department for three years, during which time I designed covers, interiors, and marketing materials.
