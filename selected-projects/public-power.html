Selected Projects

Public Power

About

Public Power NY, a statewide coalition of organizers, unions, and scientists, spent four years campaigning for the passage of the Build Public Renewables Act (BPRA) in order to meet the goals set out in 2019's landmark Climate Leadership and Community Protection Act (CLCPA).

My Role

I started organizing with the NYC-DSA ecosocialist working group, a member group of the Public Power NY coalition, in 2019. During the Covid-19 lockdown, I worked with the comms committee to produce Zoom programming to keep our base engaged. When things started to open back up, I worked with the group to art direct the city-level campaign's visual identity refresh ahead of a renewed push for in-person actions. In addition to the materials I produced myself, I created a style guide that was easy to interpret for designers and non-designers alike to produce mass communications.

Outcomes

In 2023, New York lawmakers passed BPRA as part of the state's annual budget. BPRA authorized the New York Power Authority—the largest state public power authority in the U.S.—to build renewable energy projects in order to meet the CLCPA’s climate goals. Many have called the BPRA a state-level Green New Deal.
